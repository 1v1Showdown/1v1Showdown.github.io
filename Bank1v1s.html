PokéBank 1v1s

Introduction

Ever wondered how 1v1 would be like if we didn't have those pesky pokemon like Mimikyu or Charizard-Mega? Or if you wanted to ban Z-moves but those evil council members just say no? Well you could try something I call PokéBank 1v1s!

PokéBank 1v1s is where you create a bank of Pokemon and try to build a 1v1 tier out of it. It can range from small 7 pokemon banks to large 30 pokemon banks. The idea is to get you set building which is really fun to do when you don't have to worry about running tech against cursekyu and beating all Dragonite sets.

What's so great with Pokebank 1v1 is that you have complete control over the tier you created. You are allowed to create the experience you want.

Ideas for PokéBanks.

Here are some ideas that you could use for your PokéBanks, the fun of it all is that you can mix and match ideas together!

  • /Randpoke [#], [Filter]: A constrained metagame landscape may breed creative set building.

  • No Mega Metagame: How will the absence of mega pokémon affect the 1v1 metagame landscape!

  • No Zen Moves: A controversal issue turns over as you can test out a Z-moveless metagame

  • No Species Clause: Who says I can't have 3 Porygon-Z?

  • Ban on Sleep: No Sleep for all! -BitterGuy26

  • Only Tier X (and under) is allowed: How about a UU based metagame?

  • Item Clause: Only one of each item is allowed, you could even restrict into one Z-move

  • Item Declause: All items are allowed, Focus Sash for everyone!

  • All Mega: Only mega Pokemon are allowed
